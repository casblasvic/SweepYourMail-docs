What is SweepYourMail?

SweepYourMail is a free Chrome extension that integrates directly into Gmail. It analyzes your inbox to show you exactly which senders are filling up your mailbox, then lets you clean up with powerful bulk actions.

Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

  • NO remote servers - We don't operate any servers
  • NO data transmission - Your emails never leave your browser
  • NO cloud storage - Nothing stored outside your device
  • NO analytics - We don't track usage or collect telemetry
  • NO third parties - Only official Gmail API communication

Privacy & Security

Read our full Privacy Policy

SweepYourMail is built with privacy as a core principle. All processing happens locally in your browser. We communicate only with official Google/Gmail APIs - never with any third-party services.

  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication via Chrome
  • All data stored locally in your browser
  • You can revoke access anytime at Google Account Settings
  • Uninstalling removes all local data
